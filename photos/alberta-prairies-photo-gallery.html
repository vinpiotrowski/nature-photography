Alberta Prairies Photos

The Alberta Prairies are close to home and offer a lot of diversity in atmosphere and mood. There's a certain feeling of freedom that comes with the open spaces, rolling hills, and endless fields. Unobstructed views of the sky provide awe-inspiring sunrises/sunsets and the proximity to the mountains means heart-pounding storms. Abandoned buildings, rusting agriculture equipment, and bleak colours can be captured to portray a darker temperament.
