Photo Galleries

Photographing Western Canada is about more than taking pictures for me. It's about the stories, experiences, and journeys of discovery in an unspoiled part of the world. In Alberta, changing your location by a hundred kilometers presents a different set of adventures and stories. My discovery of beauty and nature has grown to include other parts of the world in the travel gallery. Regardless of where my travels take me I still consider myself a student and am continually learning and exploring.

Canadian Rockies Photos

Photos of mountains, lakes, and waterfalls taken from the national and provincial parks of Western Canada. This particular gallery is rife with scenery that fits the archetypal postcard image and embodies the rugged outdoors lifestyle.
