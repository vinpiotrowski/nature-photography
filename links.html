My favorite blogs

Links to creative geniuses; some of which I've been lucky enough to shoot with.

Photo Cascadia

My favorite blog featuring work by Sean Bagshaw, David Cobb, Adrian Klein, Kevin Mcneal, Chip Phillips and Zack Schnepf (or bascially my top 6 favorite nature photographers).

Paul Zizka

Paul is a great guy and completely legit when it comes to nature photography. He carries a camera in one hand and an ice axe in the other and photographs some hard to get to places.

Dan Jurak

Dan Jurak is one of my photography heroes. He is a talented photographer that hangs out on the prairies and the frequency of his updates in unmatched.
